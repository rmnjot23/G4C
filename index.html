PITCH

Are you an explorer? Do you secretly wish you were one? Well Feed the Boat allows you to operate a submarine and travel to 5 different islands for 5 different treasure chests. But as you travel the ocean you might lock heads with nature. As beautiful as it is it might be your biggest obstacle.

THEME

The theme is represented through many aspects of the game. Firstly the game set up is built around a very tropical setting with water, plants and islands. Also the whale represents the enemy which symbolizes the force of nature within the game. Furthermore the conflict between the researcher sin the submarine and the whale is meant to give a message to player about nature and how we should learn to work with it not only for the benefit of nature and the planet but also our own since disrupting the natural equilibrium puts humans at the mercy of nature.

MY WHY

I was inspired to make a game about this theme because of how important nature is. It's great that science is advancing through research and innovations. these innovations are extremely important to our lives. But as we interfere more with the natural world its important to consider that we should work with nature to better understand it, and just nurture ourselves with nature. In the name of science however we continuously try to replace the role of nature and its spontaneous events which harms the natural world and ours as well.
